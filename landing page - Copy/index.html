TYPES OF CLAY
EARTHENWARE

Earthenware is traditionally made from ‘red earthenware’ clay or ‘red terra-cotta’. However, some earthenware clays are buff, grey, or white.

STONEWARE

Stoneware refers to a particular type of ceramics that is known for being durable, non-porous, and often having a sturdy rustic appeal.

PORCELAIN

When it is moist, porcelain clay is usually either a very pale grey color or a white/off-white color. Once fired, porcelain is normally white and can have a translucent appearance.
